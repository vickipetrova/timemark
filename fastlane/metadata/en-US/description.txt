How long has it been?

Tally Days answers that question beautifully. Whether you're counting the days since you quit smoking, tracking how long since your last dentist visit, or counting down to your wedding — Tally Days keeps time for you.

TRACK WHAT MATTERS
Create unlimited counters for any event in your life. Each counter shows exactly how much time has passed or how much time remains, displayed the way you want — in days, weeks, months, or a full year-month-day breakdown.

TWO WAYS TO COUNT
• Count up: Track how many days since something happened — your sobriety date, your anniversary, your last workout, when you started a new job.
• Count down: See exactly how long until an upcoming event — a birthday, a vacation, a deadline, a concert.

ORGANIZE WITH CATEGORIES
Group your events into categories like Life, Work, Health, and Personal — or create your own with custom colors and icons. Filter your view to see only what's relevant right now.

NEVER FORGET
Set daily, weekly, or monthly reminders for any event. Get notified when it's time to reset a counter or when a milestone is approaching. Your streaks stay on track without the mental load.

WIDGETS THAT WORK
Put your most important counters right on your home screen and lock screen. Multiple widget sizes let you display one event or several at a glance. No need to open the app — your progress is always visible.

BEAUTIFUL & MINIMAL
Tally Days is designed to be calm, focused, and distraction-free. A clean dark interface with customizable accent themes lets you make it yours without visual noise.

SYNC EVERYWHERE
Your events sync automatically across all your devices via iCloud. Start a counter on your iPhone, check it on your iPad or Mac.

SHARE YOUR PROGRESS
Proud of your streak? Share individual events with friends and family. Import events others share with you.

KEEP YOUR HISTORY
Every time you reset or log an event, Tally Days keeps a record. Look back at your complete history to see patterns and celebrate consistency.
